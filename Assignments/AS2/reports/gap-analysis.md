# Resume Gap Analysis Report

*   Deep knowledge of Java, JavaScript, Python, SQL, and C++ offers a versatile toolkit.
*   Strong expertise in front-end technologies like React.js and Next.js ensures modern web development capabilities.
*   Solid understanding of back-end technologies including Node.js, Express.js, and Spring Boot positions you for diverse projects.
*   Practical experience with cloud deployment on AWS (EC2, ECS, ECR, Cognito) and Vercel proves your ability to launch and scale applications.
*   Familiarity with databases, including both SQL (PostgreSQL, Oracle SQL, SQLite) and NoSQL (MongoDB, DynamoDB), demonstrates adaptability in data management.

## 🎯 Skill Gaps by Effort

### ⚡ Quick Wins (Do this week)
*   **TypeScript:** Add TypeScript to your resume and complete the free handbook at typescriptlang.org (~8 hrs) to bolster your application stack.
*   **GitHub Actions:** Explicitly list GitHub Actions under your CI/CD skills — hiring managers love candidates who ship reliable, automated code.
*   **UI/UX Design:** Expand on your UI/UX design project experience on your resume to highlight tangible outcomes and design improvements.

### 📅 Short-Term (Days to 2 weeks)
*   **AWS Certifications:** Obtain the AWS Certified Cloud Practitioner certification — a relatively easy cert that shows you're serious about cloud.
*   **Docker:** Deploy a personal project using Docker and document the process in a blog post or on GitHub to showcase hands-on experience.
*   **Testing (Jest, Mocha, Cypress):** Complete a quick tutorial or course on Jest or Mocha, so you can speak to Javascript testing frameworks in interviews.

### 🔧 Medium-Term (Weeks to months)
*   **Mobile Development (React Native or Kotlin):** Complete a course on React Native or Kotlin (e.g., Udemy, Coursera) and build a simple mobile app to demonstrate proficiency.
*   **CI/CD Pipelines:** Deepen expertise in CI/CD by setting up a pipeline for one of your personal projects using GitHub Actions or Jenkins.
*   **System Design:** Study system design principles (e.g., using the "Grokking the System Design Interview" course) to prepare for technical interviews.

### 🏗️ Long-Term (Months to years)
*   **Years of Professional Experience:** Continue building your portfolio with substantial projects to demonstrate practical skills, despite limited professional experience.
*   **Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science or Related Field:** Consider completing a degree completion pathway or online CS degree program like Seneca's or Athabasca University's to enhance qualifications.

## 💎 What Makes You Stand Out
Your broad technology stack and hands-on experience across front-end, back-end, cloud, and databases make you a versatile candidate. Your ability to adapt to different environments and quickly learn new technologies will set you apart in the job market. Focus on highlighting projects that showcase your full-stack capabilities and your ability to take an idea from conception to deployment.
