## Market Analysis Report

### 1. Most Commonly Required Skills

*   **Software Engineering Fundamentals** (e.g., Computer Science principles, Problem Solving, Object-Oriented Programming): ~37.5% of postings
*   **SQL**: ~25% of postings
*   **React**: ~25% of postings
*   **Mobile Development** (iOS, Swift, Android, Kotlin/Java): ~25% of postings
*   **REST**: ~25% of postings

### 2. Preferred / Nice-to-Have Skills

*   **Cloud Platforms:** AWS/Azure appear as preferred.
*   **Scripting:** Python and JavaScript frequently requested.
*   **ML/AI:** Listed as desirable for analytics roles and a general trend.
*   **UI Frameworks:** SwiftUI/Jetpack Compose are preferred for mobile roles.
*   **TypeScript:** Listed as preferred language for mobile roles.
*   **UiPath Orchestrator**: Preferred skill for RPA role.

### 3. Experience & Seniority Trends

*   **Entry-Level:** Identified in one listing.
*   **Junior:** Identified in one listing, typically requiring ~1 year of experience.
*   **Mid-Level:** Identified in one listing, expects ~3 years of experience.
*   **Senior:** Identified in two listings, expect ~5+ years of experience
*   **Experience Years:** The listed positions request 1 - 5 years of experience.

### 4. Education Requirements

*   **Bachelor's Degree:** Explicitly stated in two postings (Computer Science, IT, or related fields).
*   **Computer Science, Software Engineering, or Related Degree:** Required for some entry-level positions.
*   **Undergraduate degree in a related discipline:** Requested for the Analytics Engineer role.

### 5. Salary Range Overview

*   **Currency:** Predominantly CAD, one posting in USD.
*   **Range:** CAD $55,000 - $200,000 (USD listed as $160,000 - $200,000)
*   **Entry-Level (Software Developer):** CAD $85,000 - $95,000
*   **Junior RPA Developer:** CAD $55,000 - $70,000
*   **Analytics Engineer:** CAD $90,000 - $110,000
*   **Software Engineer:** CAD $120,000 - $180,000
*   **Software Engineer - API:** CAD $70,000 - $115,000
*   **Software Engineer - Mobile:** CAD $66,656.81 - $104,957.97
*   **Machine Learning Engineer:** USD $160,000 - $200,000
*   **Omitted Salary:** One posting did not list compensation details.

### 6. Remote / Location Patterns

*   **Onsite:** 3 postings
*   **Hybrid:** 3 postings
*   **Remote:** 1 posting
*   **Locations:** Primarily Toronto and surrounding areas (Mississauga, North York), showing a strong concentration in the Greater Toronto Area (GTA).

### 7. Common Responsibilities

*   **Development:** Designing, building, testing, and deploying software.
*   **Collaboration:** Working with cross-functional teams (product, design, other engineers).
*   **Maintenance:** Maintaining existing code, infrastructure, and systems.
*   **Documentation:** Creating documentation for APIs, processes, and workflows.
*   **Problem Solving:** Analyzing problems, identifying solutions, and debugging.
    *These recurring themes indicate that roles focus on full software development lifecycle involvement.*

### 8. Company Research Summary

*   **Variety:** The companies range from stealth startups (1851 Labs) to established enterprises (KUBRA, Gusto).
*   **Industry Diversity:** Includes software development, data analytics, healthcare technology, and mobile application development.
*   **Modern Tech Stacks:** Companies generally use modern technologies.

### 9. Notable Trends & Observations

*   **Full Stack Expectations:** Some "Software Engineer" roles blur the lines between frontend, backend, and infrastructure.
*   **Mobile Development Demand:** There's demand for mobile developers with both native and cross-platform skills.
*   **Data Skills are Valuable:** SQL proficiency is common; Python, ML/AI add value.
*   **Salary Transparency Issues:** Some companies still omit salary ranges.
*   **Toronto Tech Market:** A significant number of roles are located in the GTA, indicating a vibrant technology hub.
